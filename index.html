Mapadev Week

  • Pikachu

    HP 300

    Description

    Pikachus are small rodents, about forty centimeters long and six kilograms, with a round body, short legs, and a long tail. When male, the tail has a lightning bolt shape, but if the Pikachu is female, its tail has a heart shape at the end.

  • Charizard

    HP 800

    Description

    Generally, a Charizard is competitive. It loves battle and its competitive spirit is also notable, as it will never breathe fire on a weaker opponent unless provoked or ordered. When it gets very angry, the flames on its tail turn bluish-white.

  • Mewtwo

    HP 1200

    Description

    Mewtwo was genetically created in a laboratory, being a clone of the mysterious ancient Pokémon Mew. It is a creature with a humanoid posture but has some feline characteristics.
